Rules

  1. Everyone puts £20 in a pot (per game).
  2. People draw names out of a hat to form teams. The first name is a chicken and gets to select their fellow chicken. The fine for declining the honour of being chicken is 3 shots of gin.
  3. The 'captain' of the team (the first player drawn) must setup FindMyPhone with a chicken for tracking throughout the game (can be removed instantly after).
  4. The chicken gets the pot of money, buys the assembled teams a drink, and goes to secure itself in a pub or bar within the pre-determined boundaries of play.
  5. All the teams depart the starting pub 30 minutes after in search for the chicken simultaneously. Your drink must be finished.
  6. The chicken drinks through the pot of money. If asked why he or she is wearing a costume, the only acceptable answer is “what costume?”.
  7. The teams go in search of the chicken. If a team checks a pub that doesn’t house the chicken, they can’t move on until everyone in the team has had a drink. You have to enter the pub and post a picture of you with a pint in the group chat.
  8. When a team finds the chicken, they get to join in drinking through the pot of money.
  9. The losing teams buy a shot of gin.

    10. Notes: The chickens can track the players using FindMyPhone. If the chickens don't think you are entering the pubs quick enough, you can be awarded a gin.
